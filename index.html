THIS IS STARS INTERNATIONAL

Gerald Santiago
CEO & Founder, STARS INTERNATIONAL

Gerald Santiago began his career as a professional musician in 1981, forming a band with his friends and his brother, Egie. After completing his Civil Engineering degree, he pursued music internationally, starting as a Music Director aboard M/V Orient Princess in 1987.He went on to perform and work across various countries before joining Carnival Cruise Lines in 2004, where he later became a Music Manager until 2026.With decades of global experience, Gerald now leads STARS INTERNATIONAL in developing and connecting talented performers worldwide.

goal

To enhance and secure opportunities for qualified musicians, singers, and entertainers while delivering world-class entertainment worldwide. a love for music that moves the spirit.
